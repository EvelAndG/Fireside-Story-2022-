It was a dark and stormy night...

You stand soaking wet at the foot of a tall hill. Atop the hill stands a great mansion. Lightning strikes just behind the mansion. So cliche right?

The sound of the undead can be heard shambling in the distance. The mansion must be the source for all of this.

Fight or flight? Do you go and find these undead and make them dead again or do you run and find help?

Our hero had no fear, he decided to go it alone as he knew no one else was up to the task.

Muscles tensed and the trek towards the sound of the undead began.
